Sahyadri Super Speciality Hospital, Karad

Spread over about 100,000 sq ft area, Sahyadri Super Specialty Hospital Karad is a full fledged tertiary care Multi Speciality Hospital & has one of the best infrastructures in Southern Maharashtra. It has an imposing 4 storeyed, twin building, strategically located on the Pune-Bangalore Highway at Karad about 175km from Pune. The hospital, with its high quality care caters to the healthcare needs of people in Satara, Sangli and Kolhapur districts. It houses 150 beds, providing access to the best of Speciality & Superspeciality Medical Services, all under one roof.

Sahyadri Super Specialty Hospital Karad is the only Centre of Excellence in the Satara District for In Vitro Fertilization (IVF) and Joint Replacement. It also boasts of expertise in Minimal Invasive or Endoscopic / Laparoscopic Surgeries, Cardiology Services- Cardiac Angiographies & Angioplasties, Neuro Surgery and Multitrauma. The Radiology Dept. has Siemens Multi Slice Somatom, Sensation 16 for Non Invasive Coronary and Carotid Angiographies.

It has 15 beds dedicated for Critical Care equipped with all state of the art multipara monitors & ventilators, 7 beds for High Dependency Unit and 7 beds for NICU. It also has 5 Operation Theaters with a dedicated section for IVF Lab. The Private rooms, wards ICU and OPDs are spacious well lit and exudes a warm and caring ambience.
